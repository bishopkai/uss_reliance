These are the voyages of the U.S.S. Relaince. | Ship's Log

Recent Happenings:

  • Episode 1: "The Shakedown Run" - The crew of the USS Reliance embarks on their maiden voyage in the Lucanii Drift. Their mission: to test the capabilities of the new Andor Class Missile Cruiser. As they explore the frontier of Federation space, they encounter a vessel of unknown origin a forested geodesic dome. Upon investigation, they discover a sentient forest in dire need of help. The crew faces a moral dilemma as they balance their values and the Prime Directive while aiding the sentient trees and combating a neurodegenerative disease threatening a small insectoid species known as the Jahan !Xu. Their success leads to a crucial decision to violate the Prime Directive for the sake of two alien races on the brink of extinction. Concluding the episode, the crew finds a temporary sanctuary for the Jahan !Xu within a nearby asteroid field.

  • Episode 2: "The Anomalous Energy Signature" - Following the detection of an anomalous energy signature emanating from a nearby star system, the USS Reliance sets a course to investigate. The crew engages warp drive, and after approximately 12 hours, they arrive in the star system. Long-range sensor scans reveal that the energy signature originates from a small artificial planetoid within the system.

    Further investigation reveals that the planetoid is of highly advanced technology, unlike anything encountered before. It exhibits a metallic outer shell and a complex internal structure with multiple energy sources. Despite not detecting any biological life signs, the presence of advanced technology suggests potential interaction with artificial intelligence or other non-biological entities.

    As the crew continues their exploration, they take a closer look at the energy signature and determine that it originates from a central power source within the planetoid. While unable to determine the purpose or capabilities of the technology, it becomes evident that the planetoid may function as a power station or research facility.

    As the crew prepares to gather more information, they assemble an away team consisting of Captain Horatio Kidd, Lieutenant Yolen Takis, Chief Medical Officer Dr. Bog, and Lieutenant Commander Liam Adams. They discover that the planetoid is an abandoned communications relay station, and after reactivating it and establishing themselves as friendly, they receive an alert about the presence of multiple Klingon birds of prey in the vicinity.

    In response to the Klingon threat, the crew sets a new course to the Eta sector, leveraging the relay station to facilitate their journey and establish a more strategic position.

  • Episode 3: "The Distress Call" -The USS Reliance receives a distress signal from a type H planet located 9 light years away. The star system containing the planet consists of four planetary bodies, with the type H planet being the focus of the distress call.

    Upon arriving at the planet, the crew detects a crashed Federation vessel and identifies several humanoid lifeforms near the crash site. These individuals are armed with iron-age weapons. Communication is established with the crew, revealing that they were en route to a new station when their ship crashed. However, one crew member, Ensign Marcus, did not join the others in defending themselves and instead rose in rank within the local population, using advanced technology to manipulate the tribes. The local population refers to Marcus's crew as "Sky Demons," and their leader is known as Smith Johnson, or "Smitty."

    Captain Kidd beams Ensign Marcus aboard the ship and discusses the crew's priorities based on the information received. These priorities include retrieving the materials initially being transported by the mining ship, cleaning up the crashed vessel to avoid contamination, attending to the rest of Marcus's crew, and addressing the atmosphere's poisonous gas.

    Commander Kellan Lor-Shan assembles an away team consisting of Lt. Cmdr. Adams, Nurse Johann, and Lt. Dyna Blake to the surface. The away team benefits from boosters developed by Dr. Bog to protect them from the planet's atmosphere for a limited time. They explore the ship, stabilize the warp core, and successfully bring it back to the USS Reliance's shuttle bay.

    As the crew prepares to depart from the planet, Captain Kidd suggests leaving a navigation buoy with a warning about the atmosphere's effects on humanoids. The buoy is deployed, transmitting the warning message to incoming ships and providing access to the antidote if necessary.

    Lt. Cmdr. Rivera and Cmdr. Lor-Shan retrieve the black box from the USS Bender, uncovering details about the ship's crash and the materials it was carrying. They also discover strange phenomena on the planet, including strong magnetic fields and intense storms near the poles. Suspicious activity among the crew members regarding the warp core is noted, prompting further investigation.

    Using an ECM (Electromagnetic Countermeasures) system, the crew deduces the presence of silicate-based lifeforms at the planet's poles and an unknown facility. Scans are unable to penetrate beyond a barrier surrounding the facility.

    Further analysis reveals that the silicate lifeforms possess defensive and offensive weapons, as well as shielding. The crew observes a well-fortified structure, synthetic lifeforms, turrets, and an unusual satellite dish. A navigation beacon displaying data on the indigenous populations and synthetic lifeforms is deposited near Planet-1 (Golding's Planet) to provide free information.

    As the crew prepares to delve deeper into the mysteries of the synthetic lifeforms and the facility at the planet's poles, the episode concludes, leaving them with many unanswered questions and potential challenges ahead.

  • Episode 4: "Promotions and Preparations" -Captain Kellen Lor-Shan and the crew of the USS Reliance have arrived at Starbase Kepler following their successful mission involving the USS Bender. During a meeting with Admiral Sinclair, Captain Lor-Shan receives a promotion to captain, and Lieutenant Commander Yolen Takis is promoted to Lieutenant Commander and becomes the new Executive Officer. Lieutenant Commander David Rivera is appointed as the Chief Operations Officer.

    After the meeting, the crew gathers at a bar to celebrate, but their festivities are cut short when Captain Lor-Shan and Lieutenant Junior Grade Dyna Blake spot smugglers attempting a trade. Quick thinking and tricorder readings confirm that the smugglers are wanted criminals. Captain Lor-Shan and Lieutenant Takis confront the smugglers while Lieutenant Blake and Ensign Zyle Lor-Shan secure the exits. A hostage situation ensues, but Captain Lor-Shan manages to stun the smuggler and the hostage, neutralizing the threat.

    Following the incident, the crew focuses on ship matters. Captain Lor-Shan assists the Engineering team with the installation of Intermix Differentials, ensuring the ship's readiness for future missions. The crew looks forward to returning to active duty, and Captain Lor-Shan plans to grant them an extra day of shore leave to recover from their celebration.

    Additionally, Captain Kidd, formerly in command of the Reliance, joins the crew as part of "Project N7," a pilot program for his return as an away team operative. The captain and the crew eagerly prepare for their next adventure.
